## Summary

This category connects market selection, positioning, distribution, selling, retention, and expansion into one commercial system.

## What's in this category

- **[<span id="tam-sam-som">TAM, SAM, SOM</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/tam-sam-som)**: Size the full market, the serviceable market, and the share a company can plausibly obtain.
- **[<span id="product-market-fit">Product-Market Fit</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/product-market-fit)**: Evaluate evidence that a defined market pulls the product.
- **[<span id="positioning">Positioning</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/positioning)**: Frame the product around the right customer, alternative, and differentiated value.
- **[<span id="distribution-channels">Distribution Channels</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/distribution-channels)**: Choose how the product reaches and is purchased by the customer.
- **[<span id="sales-funnel-metrics">Sales Funnel & Pipeline Metrics</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/sales-funnel-metrics)**: Measure conversion, velocity, and coverage across the revenue process.
- **[<span id="cloud-marketplaces">Cloud Marketplaces</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/cloud-marketplaces)**: Use cloud procurement and co-sell ecosystems as a route to market.
- **[<span id="product-led-growth">Product-Led Growth</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/product-led-growth)**: Use product experience to drive acquisition, conversion, and expansion.
- **[<span id="sales-led-vs-product-led">Sales-Led vs. Product-Led</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/sales-led-vs-product-led)**: Match the commercial motion to buyer and product complexity.
- **[<span id="land-and-expand">Land-and-Expand</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/land-and-expand)**: Win a credible initial use case and expand through proof of value.
- **[<span id="customer-success">Customer Success</span>](/wiki/go-to-market/customer-success)**: Build adoption, outcomes, renewal, and expansion into the model.

## How to use this

Start with market sizing and positioning, then choose the motion and the metrics that make growth repeatable.
