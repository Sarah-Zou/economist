## What's in this category

- **<span id="discovery-sprint">Discovery sprint (10-customer qualitative interviews)</span>**: Structured customer discovery process for early-stage startups
- **<span id="win-loss-interviews">Win–loss interviews</span>**: Understanding customer decision factors
- **<span id="churn-interviews">Churn/renewal interviews</span>**: Retention and expansion insights
- **<span id="van-westendorp">Van Westendorp PSM</span>**: Price sensitivity measurement techniques
- **<span id="gabor-granger">Gabor–Granger testing</span>**: Willingness-to-pay research methods
- **<span id="conjoint-analysis">Conjoint / choice-based experiments</span>**: Feature and price trade-off analysis
- **<span id="maxdiff">MaxDiff for feature/benefit importance</span>**: Prioritizing product features
- **<span id="ab-testing">A/B/n tests on pricing pages & plan design</span>**: Testing pricing presentations
- **<span id="in-product-experiments">In-product pricing experiments</span>**: Metering and gating strategies
- **<span id="price-elasticity">Price elasticity estimation</span>**: Measuring how demand changes with price changes

This category provides customer research methods and experimentation techniques to validate pricing hypotheses, understand customer behavior, and optimize pricing decisions. Use these tools to gather qualitative and quantitative insights that inform your pricing strategy.
