## Summary

This category connects customer-level acquisition and retention economics to company-level margin, growth, and cash efficiency.

## What's in this category

- **[<span id="cohort-analysis">Cohort Analysis</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/cohort-analysis)**: Compare customer behavior and economics by start period or shared trait.
- **[<span id="customer-acquisition-cost">Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/customer-acquisition-cost)**: Measure the fully loaded cost of acquiring a customer.
- **[<span id="customer-lifetime-value">Customer Lifetime Value (LTV / CLV)</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/customer-lifetime-value)**: Estimate the gross profit from a customer relationship.
- **[<span id="ltv-cac-ratio">LTV:CAC Ratio</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/ltv-cac-ratio)**: Compare customer value with acquisition cost.
- **[<span id="cac-payback-period">CAC Payback Period</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/cac-payback-period)**: Calculate the time required to recover acquisition spend.
- **[<span id="arr-mrr">ARR & MRR</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/arr-mrr)**: Normalize recurring revenue into annual and monthly views.
- **[<span id="churn-rate">Churn Rate</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/churn-rate)**: Measure lost customers or revenue with consistent cohorts and denominators.
- **[<span id="gross-margin">Gross Margin</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/gross-margin)**: Understand the direct cost of delivering revenue.
- **[<span id="contribution-margin">Contribution Margin</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/contribution-margin)**: Measure what remains after variable costs tied to a unit.
- **[<span id="net-revenue-retention">NRR & GRR</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/net-revenue-retention)**: Separate retained revenue from expansion and contraction.
- **[<span id="burn-rate-runway">Burn Rate & Runway</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/burn-rate-runway)**: Translate cash consumption into decision time.
- **[<span id="rule-of-40">Rule of 40</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/rule-of-40)**: Compare growth and profitability using consistent definitions.
- **[<span id="burn-multiple">Burn Multiple</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/burn-multiple)**: Relate net burn to net new recurring revenue.
- **[<span id="magic-number">Magic Number</span>](/wiki/unit-economics/magic-number)**: Relate commercial spend to the recurring revenue it creates.

## How to use this

Start with CAC and LTV, then add margin, retention, and capital-efficiency metrics to understand the complete growth engine.
